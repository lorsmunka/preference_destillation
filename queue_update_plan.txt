QUEUE-BASED PIPELINE UPGRADE PLAN (v3)
=======================================

GOAL: Multi-domain distillation pipeline. Queue-based config for data generation,
      distillation data generation (Gemma logits), and training. Two domains:
      reddit comment sentiment classification + math word problem solving.


PIPELINE OVERVIEW (3 stages)
------------------------------
1. TEXT GENERATION     → Domain-specific input creation (Reddit extraction, math problem generation)
2. DISTILLATION GEN    → Gemma processes inputs, autoregressively generates with logit capture per step
3. TRAINING            → Student transformer learns from teacher logits

JSONL FORMAT (same for both domains)
--------------------------------------
reddit_comments.jsonl:
  {"text": "You think that 67 of Britons are unemployed?"}
  {"text": "Black Power Mixtapes is another great documentary"}

math_word_problems.jsonl:
  {"text": "Problem: \"Alice has 5 bags with 3 apples each. How many apples in total?\"\nA=?\nB=?\nC=A*B=?\nSolution: ?"}
  {"text": "Problem: \"Bob has $1231. He pays $299 for headphones. How much does he have left?\"\nA=?\nB=?\nC=A-B=?\nSolution: ?"}

Both are just {"text": "..."} — the distillation pipeline treats them identically.
The teacher's logits ARE the ground truth. No answer field needed.


MASTER PROMPT FOR MATH (sent to Gemma, student NEVER sees this)
-----------------------------------------------------------------
Follow this example exactly:

Problem: "Lisa has 3 bags with 5 apples each. She eats 2 apples. How many are left?"
A=3
B=5
C=2
D=A*B=15
E=D-C=13
Solution: 13

Problem: "Tom has 15 stickers. He gives away 7. Sarah has 12 stickers. Does Tom have more stickers than Sarah?"
A=15
B=7
C=12
D=A-B=8
E=D>C=False
Solution: False

{generated problem text with scaffold}

Gemma autoregressively generates the scaffold back with actual numbers.
Each token captured with logits for distillation.


MATH OUTPUT VOCABULARY (vs sentiment's ~525 tokens)
----------------------------------------------------
Tokens needed:
  - Digits: 0-9 (individual digit tokens, plus multi-digit as tokenized by Gemma)
  - Variables: A, B, C, D, E, F
  - Operators: +, -, *, /
  - Comparison: >, <
  - Symbols: =, \n, space, :
  - Words: Solution, True, False, Problem
  - Auxiliary: extra tokens for soft labels (TBD after tokenizer analysis)

Output projection layer size differs per domain → must be configurable.


MATH TEMPLATE DESIGN
-----------------------

Each template defines the problem text AND scaffold together as one unit:
  {
    "text": "{name} has {A} bags with {B} apples each. How many apples in total?",
    "scaffold": "A=?\nB=?\nC=A*B=?\nSolution: ?",
    "operation": "mul",
    "ranges": {"A": [2, 15], "B": [2, 15]}
  }

Generator fills template with random numbers+names → produces complete block:
  Problem: "Alice has 5 bags with 3 apples each. How many apples in total?"
  A=?
  B=?
  C=A*B=?
  Solution: ?

Operation types and their scaffolds:
  add:      A=?, B=?, C=A+B=?, Solution: ?
  sub:      A=?, B=?, C=A-B=?, Solution: ?        (constrain A > B)
  mul:      A=?, B=?, C=A*B=?, Solution: ?
  div:      A=?, B=?, C=A/B=?, Solution: ?        (generate B and result, derive A=B*result)
  mul_add:  A=?, B=?, C=?, D=A*B=?, E=D+C=?, Solution: ?
  mul_sub:  A=?, B=?, C=?, D=A*B=?, E=D-C=?, Solution: ?     (constrain C < A*B)
  add_mul:  A=?, B=?, C=?, D=A+B=?, E=D*C=?, Solution: ?
  sub_mul:  A=?, B=?, C=?, D=A-B=?, E=D*C=?, Solution: ?     (constrain A > B)
  add_sub:  A=?, B=?, C=?, D=A+B=?, E=D-C=?, Solution: ?     (constrain C < A+B)
  div_add:  A=?, B=?, C=?, D=A/B=?, E=D+C=?, Solution: ?     (clean division)
  cmp:      A=?, B=?, C=A>B=?, Solution: ?                    (direct comparison)
  cmp_sub:  A=?, B=?, C=?, D=A-B=?, E=D>C=?, Solution: ?     (arithmetic then compare, max 3 input vars)
  cmp_mul:  A=?, B=?, C=?, D=A*B=?, E=D>C=?, Solution: ?     (arithmetic then compare, max 3 input vars)

Comparison rules (Gemma 4B limitation):
  - Max 3 input variables
  - Max 1 arithmetic operation before comparison
  - Comparison is always second-to-last step
  - Solution is always True or False
  - ~50% True / ~50% False in generated data

Template count target (260+):
  add:       40 templates
  sub:       40 templates
  mul:       40 templates
  div:       30 templates
  mul_add:   15 templates
  mul_sub:   15 templates
  add_mul:   10 templates
  add_sub:   10 templates
  sub_mul:   10 templates
  div_add:   10 templates
  cmp:       15 templates
  cmp_sub:   15 templates
  cmp_mul:   10 templates

Number ranges — varied magnitudes with messy numbers:
  ~30% single digits (1-9)
  ~30% tens, round (10, 20, 30, ... 90)
  ~10% tens, messy (13, 47, 68, ...)
  ~15% hundreds, round (100, 200, ... 900)
  ~10% hundreds, messy (137, 299, 483, ...)
  ~5%  thousands (1000-2000, both round and messy)

  Per operation constraints:
    add:     A,B in full range
    sub:     A > B always
    mul:     keep factors small enough that result < 2000
    div:     generate divisor and quotient, derive dividend = divisor*quotient
    cmp:     ~50% True, ~50% False

Template variety comes from:
  - 60+ names (mixed gender, with pronoun mapping)
  - 30+ item categories (fruits, school supplies, toys, animals, tools, food, ...)
  - 15+ contexts (shopping, school, cooking, sports, travel, farming, building, ...)
  - Varied phrasings per template

500k unique problems from 260 templates:
  ~1920 problems per template, easily achievable with random names/items/numbers.
  Deduplication by hashing problem text.

-------------------------------------------

  {
    "domain": "reddit_comment_sentiment",
    "description": "500k Reddit comments classified by Gemma 4B",
    "model_name": "google/gemma-3-4b-it",
    "max_examples": 500000,
    "batch_size": 32
  },
  {
    "domain": "math_word_problem",
    "description": "500k math word problems solved by Gemma 4B",
    "model_name": "google/gemma-3-4b-it",
    "max_examples": 500000,
    "batch_size": 32
  }
]

Input file derived from domain: text_generation/{domain}/{domain}s.jsonl
Output dir: batches/{domain}/{sanitized_model_name}/
info.json: written at start, updated on completion with final stats.

sanitize_model_name("google/gemma-3-4b-it") → "google_gemma-3-4b-it"
